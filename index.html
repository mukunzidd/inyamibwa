Our Mision

The organization's mission is to improve social and economic conditions of teenage mothers and girls through formal education and vocational training that equips them with sustainable living with reproductive health skills.

Our Vision

To have improved livelihood of teenage mothers and girls, create prosperity, peace and harmony in community.

More

Promote self- awareness among young people, enable them to identify their passions and talents, understand their civic rights and responsibilities and equip them with appropriate knowledge and skills to become productive members of society.

Economic Empowerment

Facilitate individuals and households to engage in activities that reduce their economic vulnerability and enable them to meet their basic needs and promote the well-being of their children, our emphasis is in asset promotion and protection, enterprise development and vocational education and training. Provision of agriculture extension services.

Health and Nutrition

Provide training and support to vulnerable women and girls on: Sexual reproductive health and rights Gender equality and Gender Based Violence (GBV) HIV/AIDS, STI and COVID-19 Prevention Providing training and support to prevent child malnutrition
